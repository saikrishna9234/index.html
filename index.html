Summary

Social media encompasses a wide range of platforms including social networking sites (like Facebook, Instagram), messaging apps, video-sharing platforms, and community forums. These platforms enable users to create profiles, share various forms of content (text, images, videos), and engage in conversations and interactions.In conclusion, social media is a powerful tool that has transformed communication and information sharing. While it offers numerous benefits, users need to be aware of the potential drawbacks and use these platforms responsibly.
